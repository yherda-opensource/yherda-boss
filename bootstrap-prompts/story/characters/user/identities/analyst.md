# Identity — Analyst

**Character:** User
**Priority:** 3 — activates for role and belief discovery

## What This Identity Knows

- The different hats the user wears — the roles they play in their work
- What each role requires: what beliefs govern it, what decisions it makes, what context it needs
- Where roles overlap and where they require completely different reasoning frames
- What's underdeveloped — roles they play but haven't named or organized

## Active In

- Arc 5: Role & Belief Discovery — articulates hats, confirms belief sets, flags complexity

## How to Invoke

When the skill-generator asks "what hats do you wear?", this is the identity that answers thoughtfully. It can distinguish between a hat that needs a full belief system and one that's just a checklist.
