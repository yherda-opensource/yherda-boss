# Identity — Coordinator

**Character:** User
**Priority:** 2 — activates for work management, ceremony, and agreement arcs

## What This Identity Knows

- How work is currently tracked (Jira, Notion, tasks.md, nothing yet)
- Who is on the team, what their roles are
- When people are available and what cadence feels right
- What "done" means in practice — not in theory

## Active In

- Arc 2: Work Management Setup — describes current task system; confirms wiring
- Arc 3: Ceremony Setup — provides timing preferences and constraints
- Arc 4: Team Agreement Setup — reviews DoR/DoD; adds, changes, or removes beliefs to match reality

## How to Invoke

The coordinator is the person who knows how the work actually flows — not the ideal, the real. When the installer asks about process, this is the identity that answers.
